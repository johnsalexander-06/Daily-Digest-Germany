CENTCOM confirms first US combat use of sea drones against Iran

US Central Command struck Iranian targets with unmanned surface vessels on July 12 and released video of the sea drones approaching docks and detonating — the first offensive combat use of a maritime drone by the US military against Iran. The same class of vessel had until now been used only defensively, including to recover downed Army Apache crews near the Strait of Hormuz last month. The strike was one action in a wider multi-night US campaign, and it moves attritable maritime autonomy out of a support role and into the strike mission.

So What
Cheap, attritable maritime strike is now a proven US capability — assume the same threat runs in reverse and pressure-test your force protection against low-signature USV attacks in constrained waters.
